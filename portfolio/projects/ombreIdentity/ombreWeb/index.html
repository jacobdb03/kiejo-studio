What is Ombre?

Ombrē is a modern, sleek, adjustable light switch, that puts you in control of the lighting in your space. Easily adjust both the lighting and warmth of the lighting with one simple, tactile switch.

Why Ombre?

Ombrē gives you effortless and endless possibilities for how to style your rooms for all different needs. We believe mindful lighting is essential to creating a calmer, more intentional space — and Ombrē lets you seamlessly control both tone and shade in one switch, helping you tune into your surroundings and effortlessly set the perfect mood. Making your world less slightly less overwhelming, a little less harsh and a whole lot brighter.
