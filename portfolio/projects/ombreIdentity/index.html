Concept Project

Ombrē.

Ombrē is a lifestyle brand built around an adjustable light switch, designed for a generation that prioritises comfort, self-care, and a balanced lifestyle. The switch allows users to set the mood for any moment by controlling the shade and tone of their lighting-ranging from bright white for productivity to soft, warm light for relaxation.
